About Karo Pitch

Bridging the Gap Between Bharat's Founders and Capital


Thousands of founders are building remarkable businesses across India — in Tier-2 towns, Tier-3 cities, and far beyond the metros. But they struggle to access investors, mentorship, and visibility.

Karo Pitch bridges this gap by connecting Bharat's founders with investors through curated pitch events and a structured discovery platform.

How It Works

A simple, transparent path from application to investor meeting — built for founders, not bureaucrats.

01
Apply with Your Pitch Deck

Submit your application and pitch deck through our streamlined intake form. Takes under 10 minutes to complete.

→
02
Get Shortlisted

The KaroStartup team reviews every application carefully and selects the most promising startups for the pitch round.

→
03
Pitch Live to Investors

Present your startup in a curated, closed-room pitch event in front of real investors actively seeking deals.

→
04
Raise Funding & Scale

Close deals, secure capital, and take your business to the next level with our full investor network behind you.
